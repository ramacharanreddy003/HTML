Preparation of Dosa.

Ingredients required for preparation of Dosa.

Urad dal
Water
Salt
  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Urad dal
  • Water
  • Soda

    • Process of making Dosa.

    Dosa pan
    Ladle
  • Soak the rice,urad dal in water for at least 4-6 hours.
  • Drain the water from the soaked ingredients, and grind them.
  • Add water and salt and mix it as well.
  • Heat a non-stick dosa tawa and pour a ladle of batter onto the center of tawa and spread it in a circular motion.
  • Drizzle some oil and cook until the bottom becomes golden brown.
  • Flip the dosa using a spoon and cook the other side of the dosa.
  • Seve the hot dosa with the chutney.

    • Enjoy your homemade dosa!
