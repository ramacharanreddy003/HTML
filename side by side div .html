INDIA

THE CAPITAL OF INDIA IS NEW DELHI.

IT IS THE SEVENTH LARGEST COUNTRY BY THE AREA.

THE CURRENT PRIME MINISTER OF OUR COUNTRY IS "SHRI NARENDRA MODI".

NATIONAL FRUIT

THE MANGO IS THE NATIONL FRUIT OF INDIA.

IN INDIA THE HARVEST AND SALE OF MANGOES IS DURING MARCH-MAY.

THE SCIENTIFIC NAME OF MANGO IS "MANGIFERA INDICA".

IT IS ALSO KNOWN AS "AAM"
